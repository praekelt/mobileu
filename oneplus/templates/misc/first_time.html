Welcome

Welcome to OnePlus!

OnePlus is a maths revision game. If you keep playing every day, your marks will improve. That’s because the only way to revise maths is to DO maths!

From Monday to Friday every week, you’ll get 3 maths challenges to answer. Use a pen and paper to work out the answer yourself. Then find and select the answer that matches yours from the 3 options provided.

If your answer is incorrect, we’ll show you the fully worked solution to figure out where you went wrong. You’ll also be given a link to the page in your textbook where you can revise the section again.

You’ll earn badges for:

  • getting 3 right answers in a row
  • reaching 50 points
  • posting a great comment in the chat room etc.

You’ll also win airtime for doing well!

Every week that you complete all 15 questions with 60% or more right answers, you’ll get R5 airtime to play for free the next week. You’ll get your airtime every Monday.

Start by finding your way around:

In the menu bar at the top right corner of your screen, you’ll find the following site sections:

On Track - see which sections you’re doing well in, and which need a bit more work

Badges - see which badges you’ve earned already

Chat Groups - chat to your classmates and work together to solve difficult challenges

Blog - read interesting articles

Leaderboard - see how you’re doing compared to your classmates

Points -see how many points you’ve earned

Inbox - where direct messages are sent and received between you and us
