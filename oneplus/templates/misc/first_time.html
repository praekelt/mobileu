  • Welcome

    Welcome to OnePlus!

    OnePlus is a maths revision game. If you play consistently throughout the year your marks will improve, because as they say, “the only way to revise maths is to do maths”!

    Every weekday (Monday to Friday) 3 challenges will be unlocked for you to answer. Work them out using a pen and paper and then pick the correct answer from the 3 options given.

    If you pick an incorrect answer, you will be shown the fully worked solution, and you will be given a link to a page in the textbook where you can revise the relevant section again.

    You will earn badges for things like getting 3 questions in a row correct, achieving 50 points, and posting a great comment in the chat room. And if you complete all 15 questions in the week, and you achieve 60% or more, you will receive R5 airtime so that you can play for free the following week. Airtime is disbursed on a Monday.

    In the menu bar at the top right corner of your screen, you will find the following:

    On Track - where you can see which sections you are doing well in, and which need a bit more work.

    Badges - where you can see which badges you’ve earned already

    Chat Groups - where you can chat to your classmates, and work together to solve difficult challenges.

    Blog - where you can read interesting articles

    Leaderboard - where you can see how you’re doing compared to your classmates

    Points - where you can see how many points you’ve earned

    Inbox - where we can send you a message, or you can send us a direct message

    So now that you know what it’s all about, go on and take the first challenge by clicking the link below. Enjoy the game :)
